Overview


When a bank is taking the undertaking on behalf of an individual or corporate for the performance or payment obligation to the beneficiary of the guarantee is called Bank Guarantee. The three facilities applicants can get with the use of Bank Guarantee are, credit facility, cash facility and conditional facility.

Course Methodology

The course employs a variety of interactive tactics, such as short lectures by the consultant, case studies, and group exercises.


Course Objectives

The course revolves around Documentary Letter of Credit, and by the end of the course, participants will be able to:

  • Understand banking facilities related to trade finance
  • Understand and utilize various types of available letters of bank guarantee
  • Better negotiate contracts commercial terminologies
  • Understand trade finance best practices and fundamentals
  • Understand several types of available letters of credit
